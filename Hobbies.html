Summary

Creating new things and coming up with differents ways to do something has always been incredibly fun for me. I love being able to bring ideas to life and work through the complications that come from that endeavor. My main hobbies right now are sewing, cooking/baking and gaming. Soon though, I hope to add working on my own video games to that list and perhaps one day making something that takes off.

Sewing and Designing

I began sewing clothes as a hobby in 2018 and it quickly became a passion of mine. I am entirely self taught using patterns, online tutorials and youtube videos. In the beginning I only used patterns to learn all of the basics, but as I devloped an understanding of sewing I went from small changes in patterns, to making my own pieces. Eventually I got into draping and I learned how to make pieces entirely of my own design. I have made a variety of different clothes but my favorite thing to make is dresses.

Cooking and Baking

In my family cooking and baking was something we all did together often. Even when I was a young child I would help my mom with meal preparation or baking delicious snacks with my sisters. It's such a fun activity to do while socializing and you get to enjoy mouth watering food at the end. Sharing great recipes and making delicious food for other people always brings me great joy and experimenting with recipes can be a lot of fun.

Panang Thai Curry

This creamy, savory sauce never fails to deliver on flavor. Making the curry paste from scratch allows adjustments to spice level or other preferences. Once the paste has been made the stir fry is extremely easy to make and always tastes great.
